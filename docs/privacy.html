Gnome Slayer

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

In short: Gnome Slayer does not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to the developer. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Your settings and progress stay on your own device, and multiplayer data is exchanged directly between players.

1. Who We Are

Gnome Slayer is developed by Nikita Games, an independent developer. For privacy questions, contact developer@svitix.com.

2. Our Core Principle

We do not operate any game servers, accounts, or backend services that collect data about you. We do not receive, record, or store your personal information.

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The Game stores the following on your own computer, in your operating system's user-data directory, and never sends it to us:

  • game settings (audio, graphics, language) and key bindings;
  • save slots: hero progress, current chapter, achievements, and lore you have discovered;
  • an optional Discord Application ID, only if you choose to enter one.

You can delete this data at any time by removing the Game's save files or uninstalling the Game.

4. Data Exchanged During Multiplayer (Peer-to-Peer)

When you host or join a multiplayer session, the following is shared directly with the other players in that session, in real time, and not through any server operated by us:

  • your chosen display name;
  • your hero stats and level, taken from your local save;
  • your in-game position and actions, to synchronise the shared game;
  • text chat messages you send;
  • voice chat audio, only while you hold the push-to-talk key;
  • your IP address, which is inherently visible to the peers you connect to or host (this is a property of direct peer-to-peer connections and also applies over LAN or a VPN such as Radmin).

This information is used solely to run the shared game session while you play. We do not receive, record, or store any of it. Voice chat is transmitted live to other players and is not recorded by the Game.

5. Microphone

The Game accesses your microphone only for voice chat, and only while you hold the push-to-talk key. The audio is streamed to the other players in your session and is not saved or sent to us.

6. Discord Integration (Optional)

If you enable Discord Rich Presence, the Game sends presence information (current game mode, chapter or wave, party size, and elapsed session time) to the Discord application running on your own computer, through local inter-process communication, so that Discord can display your status. This is handled by Discord under Discord's Privacy Policy. No data is sent to us. You can turn this off at any time in the Game's settings.

7. Distribution Platform

You download the Game from itch.io. That platform has its own privacy policy governing your download and any account you may hold with it. We do not receive personal data from the platform beyond aggregate, anonymous download statistics it may provide.

8. Children's Privacy

The Game is a general-audience product. It does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

9. Data Security

Because we collect and store no personal data on our side, there is no developer-held data that could be breached. The security of the data stored locally on your device depends on your own device and operating system.

10. Your Rights

Since we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing on our side for us to access, correct, export, or delete. You retain full control over all local data on your device and can remove it by deleting the Game's files.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version is always the one published at this address, with the "Last updated" date above.

12. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at developer@svitix.com.
