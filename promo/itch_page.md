## Short description / tagline
Co-op 3D slasher: a knight versus hordes of gnomes. Story campaign, wave survival, PvP, Windows/Linux crossplay and voice chat.

## Description (содержимое страницы)

**Gnome Slayer** is a co-op 3D action game in a cute low-poly style: you are a knight, surrounded by hordes of surprisingly well-organized gnomes.

### Game modes
- **Story — Shards of the Mountain Heart**: 6 chapters, NPCs, side quests and **3 endings** that depend on your choices. Playable solo or in co-op.
- **Waves (PvE)**: survive against growing hordes, alone or with friends.
- **PvP**: an arena with ruins, first to 10 kills.

### Features
- **Windows ↔ Linux crossplay** — the host opens a single UDP port (7788), or everyone connects via Radmin VPN.
- **Voice chat** (push-to-talk) with mic selection and automatic game audio ducking, plus text chat.
- **Hero progression** — XP, levels and stat points: Strength, Vitality, Agility, Luck. Progress persists across runs in 3 save slots.
- **Revive mechanic** — pick up a downed friend while they bleed out.
- 4 locations with unique enemies: meadow, winter forest, autumn forest and a night graveyard. Day/night cycle with starry skies, sunrises and sunsets.
- Loot chests, items, potions, ragdolls, 3 difficulty levels (affect enemy count and AI).
- Interactive points of interest scattered across every map — shrines, campfires,
  wells, a bounty board, ancient ruins, standing stones, a crypt and an old
  battlefield — with dozens of lore fragments to uncover.
- Rare golden "elite" gnomes and a finisher mechanic for staggered enemies.
- NPCs "talk": dialogue types out letter by letter with a retro blip sound.
- 19 achievements to unlock.
- English, Russian and Ukrainian languages.
- Discord Rich Presence out of the box.

### Controls
WASD — move · LMB/RMB — attack/block · Space — roll · E — interact · V — voice · T — chat · C — stats
